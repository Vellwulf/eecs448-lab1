Once upon a time a student tried to write a story for a lab project. After several seconds of deep consideration, the student gave up and decided to just write something. The student returned to the story to make a change as per the lab writeup. Suddenly, the student was slammed with an earth-shattering, expectation-subverting, unimaginably terrifying epiphany: he wasn't writing a story at all. He was just narrating exactly what he was doing

The End
